What

Adoptr is built to aggregate animal data from shelters around the UK. Inspired by Battersea, we aim to provide the user a quicker, clearer way to see which animals are available for adoption at any one time, while adding accessible links for donations to come in to the shelters themselves.

How

  • Scala
  • Play
  • Heroku
  • Python
  • S3 Amazon Webservice
